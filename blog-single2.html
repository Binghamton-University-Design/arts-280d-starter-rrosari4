Journey to Design

There is more to design than what meets the eye. It is definitely easier said than done.

Devine Design

I never thought about being a designer, but then again that depends on what you classify as a designer. I always wanted to start my own business and in a sense that has to be designed, so maybe it has always been my dream to design.

Junior year is when I declared graphic design as my minor because I felt like it would be a good tool for me in my future marketing ventures, and creating a brand for when I decide to start a business. I also needed a creative outlet since school in general tends to not boost my creative juices naturally. It has been just read this and write that.

When I got into graphic design I really di not realize all that goes into designing different things. You need to download a lot of programs, and have the right technology to stay up to date designing material. It all starts with a paper and a pen to write and draw out your ideas, so that you can plan out how you will get to the end result. But after you plan there is still a lot of testing that needs to be done because as you go you realise some things work better even if it wasn't what you originally planned. These are the many reasons why I love design, it almsot reminds me of doodling; I always start out with one shape in mind and then something new pops through.
